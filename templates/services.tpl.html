HR Healthcheck

WhiteTree HR can provide you with a HR Health check which is an independent, objective, impartial review of your policies and procedures. This enables you to highlight and mitigate any risk, reduce cost, improve and drive performance through your people, ultimately adding value to your organisation. Taking into account the complexities of employment legislation as well as government updates, we will review and assess your current policies and procedures and report back to you clearly highlighting any areas of improvement, potential exposure, legal compliance and actions you can take. Our approach is always bespoke to fit the needs of your dynamic business and we tailor our solutions packages allowing you to pick and mix to meet your exact requirements.

  • Our free HR Healthcheck provides an initial guide to where you are with your HR requirements
  • Further to this we can ensure that you are up to date with current employment legislation and are legally compliant with a review of your current policies and procedures with a clear and detailed report of recommendations
  • Policies, procedures, terms and conditions along with employee handbooks are available from WhiteTreeHR
  • Annual updates as and when changes come into place via the government along with any changes in employment legislations to ensure that you stay compliant
