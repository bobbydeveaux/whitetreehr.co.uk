Operational HR

Assisting businesses with the day to day management of their HR so that your business can focus on what you do best its our specialism here at WhiteTree. We cover a broad spectrum of generalist HR and can support you on all aspects, whether it be occasional support or fully outsourced, we are here to help you.

Strategic HR

Working with businesses on Talent Management, Reward Management, Employee Engagement and Retention, Organisational Structure, Acquisitions and Mergers including TUPE.

Project HR

Collaborating with businesses on HR projects within their organisations, working with you from concept to completion. WhiteTree HR can also review projects, analyse and improve existing projects or aid development of ongoing projects within the business.

HR Administration

Being HR Administration experts here at WhiteTree HR we can work with your business to alleviate this time consuming, yet integral part of HR so that your time is freed to focus on business essentials without having the worry of everyday necessities.

HR Systems and Data Analytics

We are specialists in HR Software and the management of all of your employee data as well as HRIS Management and Employee Self portals. We meet ‘best practice’ and current employment legislation standards. We pride ourselves at being at the forefront of technology in this area. We are able to help with all of your Management Information, Key Performance Indicators and data analysis needs.

HR Healthcheck

WhiteTree HR can provide you with a HR Health check which is an independent, objective, impartial review of your policies and procedures. This enables you to highlight and mitigate any risk, reduce cost, improve and drive performance through your people, ultimately adding value to your organisation. Taking into account the complexities of employment legislation as well as government updates, we will review and assess your current policies and procedures and report back to you clearly highlighting any areas of improvement, potential exposure, legal compliance and actions you can take. Our approach is always bespoke to fit the needs of your dynamic business and we tailor our solutions to your exact requirements.
