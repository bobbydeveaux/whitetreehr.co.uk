Experience Counts

White Tree HR was founded by Stephanie DeVeaux in 2016. Stephanie has 12 years experience as a HR Generalist working for a wide variety of companies from Start Ups & SME’s to large national, international and global companies. She has worked within a number of industries from Manufacturing to Legal, Government to SME’s and local businesses.

During her time in HR, Stephanie has headed up a number of HR projects, worked closely with a variety of HR Systems and integrated extensively within businesses at both an Operational and Strategic level. She provides business Managers and the board with a comprehensive and professional HR service along with ensuring companies work within current employment legislation and to best practice standards.
